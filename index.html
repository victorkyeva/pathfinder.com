About Us

Our mission is to build the future of indoor navigation in Africa. We are committed to developing innovative AI and mapping solutions that make every indoor journey seamless and stress-free.

We work with building managers, facility planners, and business owners to solve the complex challenges of wayfinding in large venues like airports, hospitals, corporate campuses, and shopping malls.

Our vision is to empower people to navigate any indoor space with the same ease and confidence they feel with outdoor GPS.

Our Story & Vision

Pathfinder was born from a simple observation: while outdoor navigation has become effortless with GPS, finding your way around large, complex indoor spaces remains a challenge. We saw people getting lost in busy malls, missing appointments in vast hospitals, and struggling to find their way in corporate campuses.

Our team of innovators and engineers set out to solve this problem by combining advanced AI, precise location technology, and intuitive design. We don't just create maps; we create intelligent, dynamic routes that adapt to real-time conditions, such as closures or crowded areas, to ensure a smooth journey.

Today, our vision is to be the leading indoor navigation platform, not just in Africa, but globally. We are dedicated to continuously improving our technology to make every indoor journey as simple, efficient, and enjoyable as possible, unlocking the full potential of smart buildings.
